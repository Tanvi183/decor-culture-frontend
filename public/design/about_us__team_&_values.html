A note from our founders

Refining Daily Rituals

When we started Home and Beyond, we had one simple goal: to create a space where beauty meets utility. We noticed a gap between high-end design that felt untouchable and functional products that lacked soul.

Our journey has taken us from tiny ceramics studios in Japan to textile weavers in the Highlands. Every piece in our collection is there because it serves a purpose and inspires a moment of calm in a chaotic world.

Thank you for letting us be a small part of your home and your life. We hope these pieces bring you as much joy as we felt in discovering them.

Evelyn & Thomas
Co-Founders, Home and Beyond
