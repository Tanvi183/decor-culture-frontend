Our Story: Crafting Comfort for Every Home

Archival: The first concepts for the 'Linden' chair, 2010.

The Beginning of
Slow Living

In 2010, Home and Beyond started in a sun-drenched attic studio with a single workbench and a radical vision: to slow down. Founders Elena and Marcus saw a world moving too fast, producing objects without souls.

They believed that the objects we surround ourselves with should be more than just functional; they should be keepers of stories, crafted from materials that age gracefully alongside the families they serve.

Honoring the Craft

Every piece in our collection passes through human hands. We don't believe in shortcuts—only in the time-tested techniques of master woodworkers and weavers.

Selected Timber

Sustainably sourced walnut and oak from heritage forests.

Traditional Joinery

Dovetail and mortise-and-tenon joints that last generations.

Natural Fibers

Hand-spun linens and organic cottons dyed with plant-based pigments.

Our Journey

2010

The Spark

Founded in a small sun-drenched workshop in London, focused on one-of-a-kind commissioned pieces.

2012

The First Collection

Our 'Linen & Oak' series launched, marking our transition from custom commissions to a full residential line.

2018

Flagship Studio

Opened our global headquarters—a space for designers to gather, collaborate, and experience 'Slow Living'.

Today

100 Locations & Beyond

With a presence in 15 countries, we remain committed to the same craftsmanship that started in that attic studio.

The story continues with you.

Bring a piece of our heritage into your home. Each object is a silent partner in your life's best moments.
